About Us


At ExtravaganzaFest, we believe that life is meant to be celebrated. We are passionate about creating a vibrant and inclusive community where people from all walks of life can come together and enjoy the magic of carnival.

Our team is made up of dedicated individuals who share a love for all things festive. From designing stunning floats to curating exhilarating performances, we strive to bring you the best carnival experience possible.

Whether you're a first-time visitor or a seasoned carnival-goer, we welcome you to join us in celebrating the joy and excitement of life. Come and experience the energy, the colors, and the music that make ExtravaganzaFest a truly unforgettable event!
